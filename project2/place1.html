Light House

The Cape Bolinao Lighthouse, perched on the rugged cliffs of Patar in Bolinao, Pangasinan, is a beacon of historical significance and scenic beauty. Constructed in 1903 by American engineers, this 27-meter tall lighthouse has faithfully guided countless ships through the treacherous waters of the South China Sea and the Lingayen Gulf for over a century. Its strategic location not only ensures maritime safety but also offers visitors breathtaking panoramic views of the azure sea and the pristine coastline. As a beloved tourist destination, the lighthouse stands as a testament to the area's rich heritage and enduring charm, drawing both history enthusiasts and nature lovers to its picturesque surroundings.
